FAQ

MonSTAR is an online platform designed for Monash University students to browse, review, and share feedback on academic units, helping others make informed decisions.

1. To be able to write a review you must first login with your Monash Google Account
2. Go to the explore page and find a unit you want to review.
3. Click on the 'Review' button.

To update your review, go to your profile then go to the reviews tab, you can update or delete it there.

MonSTAR was developed by a team of students from WIRED Monash.
